Pão de Queijo Recipe

I can already smell the deliciousness!

The easy cheese bread recipe is a typical Brazilian recipe, widely consumed throughout the country and loved by all Brazilians. Its origin is uncertain, but it is speculated that it appeared around 1860 and only became popular in the 1950s. It is a delight that is part of the gastronomic history of Brazil!

Ingredients:

  • 500 g of sweet flour
  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 tablespoon/heavy salt
  • 1 package grated parmesan cheese
  • full plate (350 g) of semi-cured cheese and/or grated mozzarella (the more cheese, the tastier the bread will be)

Preparation:

  1. First, put the milk and oil in a pan to heat, turn off the heat immediately as soon as it starts to boil (you will see some milk balls rising).
  2. In a large bowl, place the flour and salt, and mix well, then pour the contents of the pan while still hot, mix well, first with a spoon and then with your hand.
  3. Then put the grated cheese and a little of the cheese on the plate and also 1 egg, keep mixing well.
  4. Put the rest of the cheese and check that the dough has a good texture, not too oily and not too dry.
  5. If you feel it's too dry, add another egg, if it gets oily, add a little more flour.
  6. This dough should release from the bowl and also from your hand.Try the dough and see if it is salty, some people like to add a little more salt.
  7. Now just make balls and place them on the baking sheet, leaving a small space between one loaf and the other.It is not necessary to grease the pan.
  8. Leave in the oven at medium temperature (230°) until golden brown.

There you have it! Enjoy your delicious meal!
